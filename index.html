• Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Mushroom
  • Contains gingerol, which has powerful medicinal properties.
  • Can treat many forms of nausea, especially morning sickness.
  • May help with weight loss.
  • Can help with osteoarthritis.
  • May drastically lower blood sugars and improve heart disease risk factors.
  • Can help treat chronic indigestion.

The known history of ginger dates back about 5000 years. Its native home is debated but its medicinal and spiritual uses were first documented in Southeast Asia, India and China. Like many other spices, ginger was once a costly commodity. In the 14th century, a pound of ginger cost as much as one sheep!
